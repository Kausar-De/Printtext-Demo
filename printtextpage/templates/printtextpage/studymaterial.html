Study Material

A study Material is described as an aid used by institutions and students that help facilitate lessons into a single and organized composition. It aims to provide students with an opportunity to get creative with their study habits and reduces the amount of information they need to learn. When a student gets too overwhelmed this lessens their ability to process and remember their lessons. Study guides, depending on how these are organized can help create mental associations that can help them remember important details. This is particularly important during examinations.
