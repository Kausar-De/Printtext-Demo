Letterhead Printing

A letterhead is a stationery design that unifies your printed correspondence to your brand. Technically, letterheads are used for printed letters but can also be used in digital messaging to maintain a branded feel. In some cases, companies use letterheads for important correspondence only. For example, a university sends emails for regular communication but uses a letterhead sent in the post for an acceptance letter. A law firm uses email daily but sends a letter on a letterhead as an official communication with a higher official. A letterhead includes content like:

  • A person or business name

  • An address

  • A phone number

  • An email

  • Contact details

  • A company logo/brand logo

  • Occasionally a background or border design
