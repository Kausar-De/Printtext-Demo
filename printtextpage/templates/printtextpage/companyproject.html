Company Project Printing

After the feasibility studies are carried out, a detailed project report (DPR) is required to be drawn up based on the data and results obtained from the studies. The preparation of the DPR is the final and most important stage of pre-investment phase of project. It is on the basis of the DPR, the Project Investments Board and the Cabinet Committee of Economic Affairs give their clearance for the project. The project report will be prepared for a plan of action to be undertaken which covers various aspects viz.,

  • Technical

  • Financial

  • Marketing

  • Management

  • Social
